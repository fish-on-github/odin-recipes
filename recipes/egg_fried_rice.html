Egg Fried Rice

Description:

Make a tasty egg fried rice in 10 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (refrigerated)
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Spring onion

Spice List:

  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Soy Sauce
  • Hot sauce or hot spices

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a wok
  2. Dice onion and garlic. Add to wok
  3. Heat for a few minutes, then remove from wok
  4. Add rice to wok. Heat and stir continuously.
  5. Heat for several minutes until rice turns golden brown
  6. Make small hole in center of wok. Crack 3 eggs into wok.
  7. Let egg set, then chop and stir.
  8. Add onion, garlic, spring onion, and spices
  9. Heat briefly, then serve.
  10. Enjoy!
