Macaroni and Cheese

Description:

Make a simple and easy Macaroni and Cheese!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups uncooked macaroni
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cream

Spice List:

  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Hot sauce or hot spices

Steps:

  1. Boil water in large pot
  2. Add macaroni and boil for 10 minutes
  3. Drain macaroni and add back to pot on medium-low heat
  4. Add milk, cream
  5. Add spices
  6. Add cheese and stir continuously
  7. Serve and garnish with some more shredded cheese
  8. Enjoy!
