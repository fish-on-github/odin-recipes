Whole Chicken Instant Pot

Description:

Make a juicy 6lb whole chicken in the Instant Pot in just 36 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Chicken - 6 lbs
  • (Optional) 1 Onion
  • (Optional) 1 lemon
  • (Optional) 4 garlic cloves
  • (Optional) Salt and Spices

Optional Spice List:

  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Old Bay
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Rosemary
  • Parsley
  • Hot sauce or hot spices

Steps:

  1. Prepare whole chicken. Remove innards.
  2. Prepare Instant Pot with metal rack and 2 cups of water
  3. (Optional) Cut lemon and onion in quarters. Stuff into chicken insides.
  4. (Optional) Stuff 4 garlic cloves into chicken insides.
  5. Place whole chicken into Instant Pot.
  6. (Optional) Sprinkle 2 tbs of Salt onto chicken.
  7. (Optional) Sprinkle 1 tbs of each listed Spice onto chicken.
  8. (Optional) If using onion and garlic, omit Garlic Powder and Onion Powder.
  9. (Optional) Add hot sauce if desired.
  10. Close Instant Pot lid and SET VENT TO SEALED.
  11. Click "Pressure Cook" and set timer to 36 minutes.
  12. Timer is calculated by 6 mins times weight of chicken. (6*6=36mins)
  13. Unclick "keep warm" setting.
  14. Wait for pressure cooker to shut off.
  15. DO NOT VENT STEAM
  16. Let pressure cooker cool down naturally while leaving lid sealed.
  17. When cooled, steam vent will open. The chicken is ready to cut and be served.
  18. Enjoy!
